# Language Selection Guide

## How to Switch Languages During a Call

When you call the health screening number, you'll hear language options in all 10 supported languages.

### Available Languages & Keys:

1. **Press 1** - English
2. **Press 2** - Hindi (हिंदी)
3. **Press 3** - Tamil (தமிழ்)
4. **Press 4** - Telugu (తెలుగు)
5. **Press 5** - Bengali (বাংলা)
6. **Press 6** - Marathi (मराठी)
7. **Press 7** - Gujarati (ગુજરાતી)
8. **Press 8** - Kannada (ಕನ್ನಡ)
9. **Press 9** - Malayalam (മലയാളം)
10. **Press 0** - Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ)

**For Punjabi specifically:** Press **0** on your phone's keypad when you hear the language selection menu.

### What Happens:

1. You call the number
2. System greets you in English and Hindi
3. You hear all 10 language options spoken in their native languages
4. Press the number corresponding to your preferred language
5. The entire screening (questions, instructions, results) will be in that language
6. You'll also receive WhatsApp/SMS reports in the same language

### If You Don't Select:

- After 10 seconds with no input, you'll hear the options again
- After 2 attempts, the system defaults to Hindi
- You can always call back to select a different language

### For Health Workers:

- Press ***** (star key) to access ASHA worker mode

## Translation Status

All 10 languages have complete translations for:
- ✅ Greetings and instructions
- ✅ Health screening questions
- ✅ Results and recommendations
- ✅ Medical advice
- ✅ WhatsApp/SMS reports

The system uses Google Neural voices for natural-sounding speech in each language.
