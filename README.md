The objective of the hackathon was to encourage students to design and prototype **AI-powered solutions for inclusion**, with emphasis on:

- AI for Financial Literacy to Rural People
- Multilingual and Multimodal Accessibility Tools
- AI Tutors for Schools
- Bias-Free AI for Recruitment
- AI for Energy Saving
- Open Challenge: AI for Inclusion
